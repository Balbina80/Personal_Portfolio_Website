About

Websites built with clarity, style and real business purpose

I’m Barbara — the person behind BJ Studio. I create websites for small businesses, personal brands and service-based clients who want a stronger and more professional online presence.

My approach combines thoughtful structure, responsive layouts and attention to detail with a practical understanding of business needs. I focus on creating websites that not only look elegant, but also build trust, improve visibility and support growth.

Every project is designed with care, clarity and strong visual direction to help your brand feel professional, polished and memorable online.
